About WaveViz

Your interactive companion for understanding electrical waveforms

What is WaveViz?

WaveViz is an interactive web application designed to help students, engineers, and electronics enthusiasts visualize and understand different types of electrical waveforms. Whether you're studying signal processing, working on circuit design, or just curious about how different waveforms behave, WaveViz provides an intuitive platform to explore these fundamental building blocks of electrical engineering.

Our tool allows you to manipulate key parameters like frequency, amplitude, and phase in real-time, giving you immediate visual feedback on how these changes affect the waveform shape and characteristics.
